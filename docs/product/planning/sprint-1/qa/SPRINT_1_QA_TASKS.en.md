# Sprint 1: QA Tasks

## Sprint Objective
Validate whether Sprint 1 onboarding is understandable, safe, and aligned with business rules before it is treated as ready for demo and MVP continuation.

## Scope
- onboarding usability testing
- product lock and unlock rule validation
- wallet state validation
- `Agent Wallet` state validation
- loading, error, success, and blocked message validation
- evidence and recommendation consolidation for PO, dev, and design

## Final Sprint Deliverables
- onboarding usability checklist executed
- onboarding business-rules checklist executed
- issue log by severity
- objective recommendations for dev, design, and PO

## Task Q1.1: Validate onboarding usability
- Objective: verify whether a non-technical user understands the onboarding flow and can complete the journey without major ambiguity.

## Task Q1.2: Validate onboarding business rules
- Objective: confirm the product respects the Sprint 1 lock and unlock rules.

## Task Q1.3: Validate messages, states, and recovery
- Objective: ensure loading, error, success, and blocked states guide the user to the next action.

## Task Q1.4: Consolidate the Sprint 1 QA report
- Objective: turn QA findings into actionable output for sprint closure.
